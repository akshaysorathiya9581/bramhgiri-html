Party Lawn and Amphitheatre

Description

The resort has plenty of amenities for every occasion. Our party lawn and adjoining amphitheater are ideal for hosting private events. The party lawn is a perfect setting for family functions and festivities, as well as corporate parties.

The amphitheater is spacious with an adequate seating capacity. It could be the right venue for arranging company town halls, private concerts and cultural programmes. Attendees can savour the peaceful, natural surroundings and soak in the experience of your event.
