Rooms & Villas

Brahmagiri has been blessed with adequate facilities for excellent accommodation. We have special packages for your stay in either rooms or villas. The villas enjoyed a breathtaking view of the valley and are available in one-bedroom or two-bedroom versions. Our rooms and villas are equipped with all modern amenities, that have been installed to make your stay comfortable. Adequate staff are at your service throughout the day, should you require anything at all.

The resort takes on a different look and feel according to the seasons. It is very beautiful in the monsoons and takes on a completely different garb during the autumn and winter months.
