Discover peace, serenity and nature

If you’re thinking of combining adventure along with leisure, set in the serene, scenic beauty of the Kokan, look no further than Bramhagiri.

Of course, the Konkan region needs no introduction. It is famous for its beautiful sparkling beaches with unpolluted clear blue waters. These magical beaches can be easily accessed from locations like Ganapatipule, Ratnagiri, Harihareshwar and Sindhudurg, to name a few.

If you’re thinking of combining adventure along with leisure, set in the serene, scenic beauty of the Konkan, look no further than Brahmagiri. It is one of the most beautiful resorts in Konkan, spread over a breathtaking 45 acres of greenery, surrounded by lush green hills and mountains
